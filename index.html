About Acadable

From Courses to Codebases

Launched in 2022 as Classmate Teachers, we rebranded to Acadable in December 2022. Our journey grew from exam prep and spoken English to becoming a full-stack tech learning platform.

In January 2024, we launched Programming++, a vibrant student tech community across 3 campuses and 3000+ learners. Now, we're building open-source projects with real-world impact—student-led, Acadable-powered.
